### Our Story

Orient Rubber Products was established in 1998 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, with a clear objective to manufacture dependable rubber components for industrial applications where consistency and reliability are critical. From the beginning, the focus has been on maintaining process discipline, understanding customer requirements, and delivering components that perform reliably in real-world applications.

Over the years, we have developed into a fully equipped manufacturing unit located at SIDCO Industrial Estate, Thirumudivakkam. With all major production processes handled within our facility, we maintain control over quality, timelines, and customization. This enables us to support both development requirements and ongoing production with equal efficiency.

Today, we serve clients across automotive, kitchen appliance, medical equipment, electrical, and industrial sectors across India. We continue to position ourselves as a reliable rubber components manufacturer in Chennai, supporting customers across India with consistent quality, responsiveness, and long-term reliability.
